The recent decline in Alphabet's stock, as highlighted in the introduction, may adversely impact investor sentiment despite the company's promising advancements in AI, which drive product usage and efficiency. Over the past five years, the company's total shareholder return, including share price and dividends, was 129.83%. This longer-term performance shows resilience despite recent market volatility. Despite assumptions of 11% annual revenue growth and $136.3 billion in earnings by April 2028, external factors like geopolitical challenges could pose risks to these projections. The 8% drop in share price narrows the gap but still leaves the current price, US$157.07, substantially below the consensus price target of US$217.319.
