He was named COO in October 2023, widely seen as a precursor to his elevation to CEO.Analysts highlighted Creed’s leadership in Energy & Transportation as key to Caterpillar’s evolving strategy. Barclays noted investor focus on the segment has grown alongside demand for data centers and power generation, where Caterpillar sees potential for competitive differentiation.Umpleby’s tenure is credited with restoring stability and profitability through a renewed focus on core businesses.
